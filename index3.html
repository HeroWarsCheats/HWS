How to simulate boss battle

Simulation Steps
Step 1: Open HeroWarsSimulator
You need to go on simulation battle

Step 2: Guild Raid
You need to go on the mode you want to test.
Step 3: Select Player
by name
or by game ID
both works
Step 4: Choose your team
select heroes
and pets you want to use
you can also modify them
then confirm your choice
Step 5: Select Boss
You should chose boss
and level of the boss
Step 6: Select BUFFS and start battle
You should select the right buffs
then you can start manual battle
then if your game is open
a manual battle will start
you will see the score when it ends
for now it's all...
